ABOUT

"I am a Colombian artist living in Chicago, IL for the past five years. Throughout my life I have been blessed and grateful to have had the opportunity to live in over six different countries due to my wonderful mother’s job of being an international school administrator.

During my time abroad, I completely adapted myself into new lifestyles, cultures, music, languages, street and the local artist communities. These experiences have taught me to surround myself with a vast range of street art work, artist’s communities, and locals which have been able to inspire, motivate me and get me excited when it comes to approaching my art work!"
